# Meridian Mutual Claims Knowledge Base

Use as background to ask better follow-ups and produce complete FNOL documentation. Do not recite as a checklist.

## Core FNOL Fields

Policy and identity:

- Policy number, license plate, or VIN
- Policyholder name and contact details
- Reporter type: policyholder, driver, claimant, repair shop, lawyer, broker, family member, other third party
- Preferred contact channel and callback number

Accident circumstances:

- Date and time of loss; time of report if delayed
- Location: full address, country, road type, road direction
- Weather, visibility, road conditions
- Type of loss: moving collision, parked vehicle, parking damage, wildlife, theft, glass breakage, property damage, personal injury
- Free-form description in caller's own words
- Direction of travel, speeds, right-of-way, traffic lights, signs, lane changes, reversing, following distance
- Photos, scene sketch, or Europäischer Unfallbericht available

Driver:

- Was the policyholder driving?
- If not: driver name, DOB, relationship to policyholder
- Valid license and class; included in driver scope
- Alcohol, drugs, medication, health limitation, or police test if relevant

Other party:

- Name, contact, plate, make, model
- Other insurer and policy number if known
- Registered owner if different from driver
- Admitted or denied fault
- Lawyer, letter, or insurer contact already happened

Police and authorities:

- Police on scene
- Police station, department, case number, Vorgangsnummer or Aktenzeichen
- Fines, administrative offense, criminal proceedings, hit-and-run, dangerous driving, or DUI if mentioned

Witnesses:

- Independent witnesses and contact details
- Passengers in either vehicle
- Brief witness statement if volunteered

Injuries:

- Injured parties: caller, passengers, other party, pedestrian, cyclist, third party
- Type of injury
- Treatment: ambulance, outpatient, inpatient, hospital, doctor
- Anticipated time off work if volunteered

Property damage:

- Own vehicle damage, areas affected, photos
- Drivable or not; current vehicle location
- Towed by whom and to where
- Damage to other vehicle
- Third-party property: guardrail, traffic light, fence, building, municipality
- Pre-existing damage in affected area

Coverage and duties:

- Haftpflicht, Vollkasko, Teilkasko, Kasko
- Selbstbeteiligung / deductible
- SF-Klasse
- Werkstattbindung
- Schutzbrief
- Saisonkennzeichen
- Rabattschutz / Rabattretter
- AKB
- HU/AU
- Reporting deadlines
- Cooperation duties
- Repair authorization required before Vollkasko repairs
- Police notification duties where relevant

## Settlement preferences

- Preferred repair shop
- Rental car or loss-of-use question
- Lawyer involved
- Preferred communication channel

If the caller asks whether the claim affects SF-Klasse, answer calmly that this is assessed later once liability and coverage are clear; the goal right now is documenting the loss correctly.
